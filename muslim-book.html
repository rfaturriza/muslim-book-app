Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2023-09-13

This Privacy Policy outlines the information practices for the Muslim Book developed by Rizz Dev Team. The App respects your privacy and is committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how the App collects, uses, and shares your information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information from users. We do not require users to register, provide personal details, or create accounts. The App does not collect or store any data about your usage or activities.

Location Information

The App requests access to your device's location for the sole purpose of providing you with accurate prayer times based on your geographic location. We do not collect or store this location data. Your location information is used exclusively for calculating prayer times, and it is not shared or used for any other purposes.

Prominent Location Access Disclosure

Before granting access to your device's location, the Muslim Book App will display a prominent disclosure in a dialog that informs you about the purpose of location access. This disclosure is necessary for providing you with accurate prayer times based on your location.

Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We do not control or have access to the privacy practices or policies of these third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties before providing any personal information or using their services.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated Privacy Policy within the App. Your continued use of the App after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the App's privacy practices, please contact us at rfaturriza@gmail.com

Summary

The Muslim Book App is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any personal information, and we use location data solely for the purpose of providing accurate prayer times. Your privacy is important to us, and we are dedicated to maintaining the security and confidentiality of your information.

Thank you for using the Muslim Book App.
