Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines the information practices for the Muslim Book developed by Rizz Dev Team. The App respects your privacy and is committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how the App collects, uses, and shares your information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information from users. We do not require users to register, provide personal details, or create accounts. The App does not collect or store any data about your usage or activities.

Location Information

The App requests access to your device's location for the purposes of providing you with accurate prayer times based on your geographic location and fetching nearby study information. We do not collect or store this location data. Your location information is used exclusively for these purposes, and it is not shared or used for any other purposes.

Advertising ID

The App may use your device's advertising identifier for the purpose of showing ads. The advertising identifier is a unique ID associated with your device, which helps in delivering more relevant and personalized ads. This information is used in accordance with the advertising platform's policies and is not shared with third parties for any other purposes.

Permissions and APIs that Access Sensitive Information

Before granting access to sensitive information, the Muslim Book App will display a prominent disclosure in a dialog that informs you about the purpose of accessing sensitive information. This disclosure is necessary for providing you with accurate prayer times and nearby study information based on your location.

Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We do not control or have access to the privacy practices or policies of these third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties before providing any personal information or using their services.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated Privacy Policy within the App. Your continued use of the App after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the App's privacy practices, please contact us at rfaturriza@gmail.com

Summary

The Muslim Book App is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any personal information, and we use location data solely for the purposes of providing accurate prayer times and fetching nearby study information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are dedicated to maintaining the security and confidentiality of your information.

Thank you for using the Muslim Book App.
